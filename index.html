About Me

I recently completed my PhD from the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi, under the supervision of Prof. Gourab Ghatak. My research focus during my PhD was on developing tools from Stochastic Geometry and applying them in modelling, analysis, and optimization of wireless and automotive radar networks. Right at the end of the 3rd year in my PhD, I got interested in problems related to multi-armed bandits and learning theory. This led me to work on Sharpe ratio optimization using bandit algorithms.

This is why I am looking for PostDoc positions in MABs and online learning.

Research Interests

My current research interests are in two directions:

  • Stochastic modelling of wireless networks
  • Multi-armed bandits and online learning
