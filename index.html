About Me

I am pursuing my PhD at the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi. My research focuses on multi-armed bandits, stochastic geometry, and applications in wireless networks and learning theory.

Research

My research contributions include:

  • Statistical analysis of automotive radar networks using Line Cox Processes
  • Optimization of bandit algorithms with Sharpe ratio objectives
  • Stochastic geometry models for wireless communication networks

Publications

  • Paper 2: Thompson Sampling with Sharpe Ratio Bandits (2024)
  • Paper 3: Resource Allocation in Wireless Networks using Multi-Armed Bandits (2023)
